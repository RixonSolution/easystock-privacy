This page explains how a user of the EasyStock Retailer or EasyStock Wholesaler app (published by Rixon Solution) can request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to request account deletion

  1. Send an email to fairtradecourt@gmail.com from the email address registered on your EasyStock account.
  2. Use the subject line: "EasyStock Account Deletion Request".
  3. Include your registered phone number and shop/business name so we can locate your account.
  4. We will confirm your identity and process the deletion within 7 business days, and reply once it's complete.

What gets deleted

  • Your name, phone number, and email address
  • Shop photo and business/identity documents (CNIC, business card) submitted during registration
  • Your shop's stock records entered in the app
  • Your connections to wholesalers/retailers

What may be retained

Completed order records (order history, quantities, amounts) may be retained in anonymized or aggregate form even after account deletion, where needed for the counter-party's (wholesaler's or retailer's) own business/accounting records, or where retention is required by applicable law. No personally identifying information is kept beyond what's necessary for these purposes.
