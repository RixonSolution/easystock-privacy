EasyStock Wholesaler

PAINT & PLY DISTRIBUTION
Last updated: 7 July 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the EasyStock Wholesaler mobile application (the "App"), developed and operated by Rixon Solution. It explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how it is protected, and how you can request its deletion.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal details — name, phone number, email address
  • Business/identity documents — business card, CNIC or business registration details, submitted during registration for verification
  • Business information — business name, address, city, brands carried
  • Stock & order data — inventory quantities, prices, order history, order status, and delivery tracking entered or generated through use of the App
  • Camera / photos — the App uses your device camera to scan paint color swatches for cataloging stock. Captured images are processed to prefill color/product details and are stored as part of your stock catalog. The camera is not used for any other purpose.
  • Device information — basic device/app diagnostics needed to operate push notifications and troubleshoot crashes

2. Why We Collect It

  • To create and verify your wholesaler/distributor account
  • To connect you with retailers so they can view your live stock and place orders
  • To operate stock management, order approval, and delivery tracking
  • To let you scan and catalog paint color swatches quickly using your camera
  • To send you push notifications relevant to your account (new orders, connection requests, subscription status)

3. How Your Data Is Used & Shared

Your stock is visible to retailers only after a mutual connection is approved inside the App — this can be revoked at any time, which also removes visibility. We do not sell your personal information to third parties, and we do not use your data for advertising.

4. Payments

EasyStock coordinates order information between you and connected retailers only. Payments are made directly between you and the retailer, hand-to-hand or via third-party services (EasyPaisa, JazzCash, bank transfer) outside the App. EasyStock does not process or store payment card or bank account details.

5. Data Security

Data is stored using Google Firebase (Firestore, Storage, Authentication) and transmitted using industry-standard encryption (TLS) in transit. Access to your data is restricted based on your role and your approved connections.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your account and order data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time — see how to request deletion. We will process deletion requests within a reasonable time, except where retention is required to comply with legal or accounting obligations.

7. Children's Privacy

EasyStock Wholesaler is a business-to-business tool intended for distributors and wholesalers. It is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect data from anyone under the age of 18.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
